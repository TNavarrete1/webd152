The Story Behind Pacific Trails Resort

How It All Began

Pacific Trails Resort was born from a passion for nature and a desire to provide visitors with an authentic outdoor experience. Founded in 2010 by Michael and Amelia Johnson, it began as a small camping site for weekend getaways with friends and family. It quickly grew into a popular destination for the local community and beyond.

Today, Pacific Trails Resort continues to offer a costal retreat where guests can disconnect from the noise of everyday life and reconnect with nature. While it has grown, the resort remains true to its roots, ensuring that every visitor has a welcoming and unforgettable experience in the heart of the Pacific Coast.

Our Mission & Values

At Pacific Trails Resort, our mission is to create welcoming outdoor experiences where guests can relax, reconnect, and make lasting memories. We're passionate about serving our community and making sure every visitor feels at home, whether they're exploring our trails, enjoying the beach, or staying in our yurts. We also care deeply about protecting the natural environment that makes this place so special, and we're committed to being responsible stewards of the coast and ecosystems for future generations. Everything we do, from how we treat our guests to how we manage the land, reflects our values of kindness, respect, and conservation.

What We Offer

Pacific Trails Resort gives guests a mix of outdoor activities and easy comfort, with packages we can tailor to your group. You can hike our coastal trails, explore tide pools, and bird watch in the morning, or paddle through calm water in a kayak and take in the skyline at sunset. If you prefer slower days, try a beach picnic, yoga on the dunes, or a guided nature walk. Back at camp, our yurts and shared fire pits are ready for casual evenings around the fire. We aim for a relaxed stay where people can slow down, enjoy fresh air, and leave feeling recharged.
